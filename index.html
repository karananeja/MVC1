Major-Project

2018-2019

Design and Fabrication of a Trainable 5 DOF Robotic Arm

New Horizon College of Engineering
Major Project

A Robotic Arm is a projection of links which is programmable and can be used to imitate a human arm.
It can do a variety of simple and complex moves. There are many platforms which can be used to control a robot like MATLAB, Arduino IDE, etc.
Arduino IDE is being used here to control the robot. This is a 5 DOF robot and the application of the arm depends on the end effector.
Pick and place robot, segregation of objects, etc are some of the applications.
